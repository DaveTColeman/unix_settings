CU Boulder VPN Access
============

## Linux

Download from [CU OIT](http://www.colorado.edu/oit/services/network-internet-services/vpn)

```
chmod 755 vpn_install.sh
sudo ./vpn_install.sh
sudo ./vpnui
```

Then ``vpn.colorado.edu`` and my identikey credentials
